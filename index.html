About Royal Enfield

Royal Enfield is an Indian multinational motorcycle manufacturing company headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The company is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production, and operates manufacturing plants in Chennai in India.

The company's origins can be traced back to 1901 when Enfield Cycle Company, of Redditch, Worcestershire, England, launched its first motorcycle. Royal Enfield has been a symbol of resilience, craftsmanship, and timeless appeal for over a century.
